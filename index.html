Corporate Mission

Established in 2026, NorthStar Express is a growing virtual trucking company built for drivers who want more than just miles on the road. We are creating a professional, active, and welcoming VTC where members can take part in convoys, represent a clean company image, and grow alongside a team that takes pride in doing things properly.

Our goal is to expand NorthStar Express into a respected name across the TruckersMP community by developing strong drivers, trusted staff, and meaningful partnerships. Whether you want to drive, help manage events, support the community, or partner with us, NorthStar is built to give motivated people a place to stand out.

Terms of Service

1. Membership Acceptance

By applying to NorthStar Express. you agree to abide by our internal regulations and maintain professional conduct within all TruckersMP and simulation platforms.

2. Code of Conduct

Drivers must operate with respect toward all other virtual trucking community members. Discrimination, harassment, or intentional disruption of simulations is grounds for immediate termination of membership.

3. Media & Assets

All NorthStar Express liveries, logos, and proprietary software are for official member use only. Unauthorized distribution of company assets is strictly prohibited.

4. Activity Requirements

To ensure a vibrant fleet, we expect members to participate in at least one official company event per month, unless prior notice is given to HR.

5. LOA Requierments

If you are in need of LOA or you know your going to be unactive for so long. We ask you to create a support ticket and let one of our HR+ Know.

5. Liveries Requirements

All NorthStar Expess drivers must use our company colours on the truck and trailer. Failer to do so could lead to a training course or derank.
